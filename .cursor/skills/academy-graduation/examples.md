## Tenure caveats (2026-07-09)

- Discord join date is the tenure proxy; corp join dates need ESI `track_members` (often expired).
- `2025-08-04` batch on Discord/character created_at is a site migration — mark `tenure_unreliable`, do not trust 338d alone.
- Migration-batch pilots need strong 90d activity to graduate on merit, not date alone.
- Pilots under 180d with unreliable dates but high activity: note in exclusions unless leadership wants early grad.
