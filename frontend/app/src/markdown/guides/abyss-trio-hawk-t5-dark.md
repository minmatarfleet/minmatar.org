## General Overview

- Initial Cost: ~240m (~80m/ship)
- Average ISK/h *after filament costs*: ~450m ISK at T5, ~900m at T6
- Difficulty: Easy / Medium

| Pros | Cons |
| --- | --- |
| High isk/h Easy once you learn the rooms Very low cost investment | Near-guaranteed deathroom in angel Very high SP-requirements |

The below skill table is merely a guide on some of the skills needed, not all of them. An assumption is made that if you attempt this above T4, you have all support skills at V for both capacitor, shield, navigation and missile skills.

Mandatory skills
 
 Assault frigate V
 Light missile Spec. IV
 All support Missile skills to V
 Any necessary fitting skills to fit the ships

## General suggestions

- Generally you want to do a 180 in each room at the beginning to give yourself more time before rats arrive.
- Ignore extra caches.
- All rooms have a "general priority" for killing, however those are not 100% true, as sometimes you get a bad spawn and will have to prioritise differently, we can't cover every situation in the guide, but will post the general priority below.
- Stay out of Blue clouds
- Stay out of White clouds
- Stay out of Red clouds
- If you can't fit everything, you're likely missing rigging skills

## The Rooms

### Drone Rooms

#### Tessella (Frigate room)

**Danger Level:** Easy  /  Medium 
 Kite

- Focus Snarecaster
- Focus Fogcasters
- Focus Lancers

Notes:
 Do a 180, kite as much as possible

#### Tessera (Battlecruiser room)

**Danger Level:** Easy 
 Kite

Notes:
 Just stay 10km away as a minimum from them and kill

#### Overmind (Battleship room)

**Danger Level:** Easy 
 Kite

- Focus Snarecaster
- Focus Starving
- Focus Spotlighter
- Kill rest
- Kill Overmind

Note:
 Make sure you keep transversal up vs overmind, and don't get within 15km to avoid web

### Triglavian Rooms

In all Triglavian rooms you will want to focus the *prefix* first, no matter shiptype, followed by the usual order of Damavik > Kiki > others

#### Kikimora (Frig/Dessie room)

**Danger Level:** Medium  / Hard
 Kite

- Focus Tangling
- Focus Starving
- Focus Ghosting
- Focus Striking

Notes:
 Kite as much as possible, try to slingshot if they get spooling

#### Vedmak (cruiser room)

**Danger Level:**Medium
 Kite

- Focus Tangling
- Focus Starving
- Focus Ghosting
- Focus rest of Damaviks
- Focus rest of Kikimoras
- Focus Vedmaks

Notes:
 Kite as much as possible, try to slingshot if they get spooling

#### Vila (Cruiser room)

**Danger Level:** Easy 
 Kite

- Focus Tangling
- Focus Starving
- Focus rest of Damaviks
- Focus rest of Kikimoras
- Focus Vilas

Notes:
 Ignore the drones, killing Vila stops the drones, or use suppression towers to kill them

#### Drekavac (Battlecruiser room)

**Danger Level:** Easy 
 Kite

- Focus Tangling
- Focus Starving
- Focus rest of Damaviks
- Focus rest of Kikimoras
- Focus Drekavacs

Note:
 Kite as much as possible, try to slingshot if they get spooling

#### Leshaks (Battleship room)

**Danger Level:** Easy 
 Approach Leshaks ASAP

- Focus Starving
- Focus Tangling
- Focus Renewing
- Focus Blinding
- Kill rest

Notes:
 Leshaks keep distance @ Tangling (12km), Starving (35km), rest (65km)

### Sleeper Rooms

#### Lucid Sleepers (cruiser room)

**Danger Level:** Easy 
 Kite

- Focus Wardens
- Focus Upholders
- Focus Sentinel
- Focus Firewatchers
- Kill the rest

Notes:
 As usual, just try to Kite while killing web/neuts first then the rest

#### Ephialtes / Tyrannos (cruiser / extras room)

**Danger Level:** Easy 
 Kite

- Focus Entanglers / Upholders
- Focus Dissipators
- Focus Tyrannos

Notes:
 Keep transversal up and try to stay out of range from Tyrannos
 Tyrannos spawns with modifiers, make sure you kill the "Entaglement" and "Nullchargers" early if so, as they web / neut

### Deepwatcher (Battleship room)

**Danger Level:** Easy  /  Hard  (Time)
 Grab cache and then orbit gate

- Kill deepwatchers

Notes:
 Keep transversal up and you can't die

#### Drifters

#### Karybdis "Karen" Tyrannos (variants)

**Danger Level:** Easy  /  Medium 
 Kite

- Focus Entanglers / Entanglement
- Focus Dissipators / Nullcharge
- Focus Illuminators
- Focus Karybdis
- Kill rest

Notes:
 if a "Ephialtes Lancer" cruiser spawns, do not kill it, Karybdis will orbit the cruiser so you can keep it around the gate / within range
 make sure to keep transversal up vs Karybdis to avoid oneshots.

### Edencom Rooms

#### Marshal

**Danger Level:** Medium  / Hard
 Kite

- Focus on Arrester
- Focus Marshals ( Drainer -> Marker -> Arrester)
- Focus on Drainers
- Kill rest

Notes:
 If there is a lot of Drainers on grid, kill those first before going on Marshals

### Pirates

#### Sansha (Frigate/cruiser mix)

**Danger Level:** Medium 
 Kite initially

- Focus Heralds
- Focus Fishers
- Focus on Priests
- Focus Smiths
- Focus Lookouts

Notes:
 You should be faster so you shouldn't be in danger, Focus down Heralds first, then Fishers to make sure you can heat out of range if you accidentally get caught out.
 If there is a lot of priests, focus those after Heralds.

#### Angels (frigate/cruiser mix)

**Danger Level:** Easy / Nightmare 
 RIP

- Focus Lucifer Cynabal
- Focus Elite Lucifer Cynabal
- Kill rest

Notes:
 This room is most likely gonna be a death room, your only option is for whoever gets aggro, to overheat their entire ship and just burn straight out into the redzone, while the other 2 hawks heat DPS on cynabals, but don't expect to live through it and just get in your next hawk.
